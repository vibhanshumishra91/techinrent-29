Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

TechInRent ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights over it.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, work email, phone number, company, and message — when you submit a form, book a call, or contact us. We also collect basic technical data (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited) via analytics cookies to improve our website.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide our services.
  • To schedule and conduct consultation calls.
  • To send relevant updates you have requested (you can opt out any time).
  • To improve our website, content, and services.

3. Legal basis & GDPR

Where applicable, we process data on the basis of your consent, our legitimate interest in operating our business, or to perform a contract. If you are in the EU/UK, you have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your data, and to object to processing.

4. Cookies

We use essential and analytics cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with trusted service providers (e.g. analytics, scheduling, communication tools) who process it on our behalf under appropriate safeguards, or where required by law.

6. Data retention & security

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete or anonymise it. We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. See our Security & Data Privacy page for more.

7. Your rights & contact

To exercise any data right, or for any privacy question, contact vibhanshu@techinrent.com.
