Security & Data Privacy

Last updated: January 2026

Security is built into how we operate. This page explains how we protect your data and your LinkedIn accounts.

1. Account safety first

Every workflow is policy-safe: we operate within LinkedIn's activity limits, use human-like patterns, and never deploy spammy automation that risks restrictions. Protecting your account health is our first priority.

2. Data protection

  • We collect only the data we need to deliver your service.
  • We use secure access methods and limit who can view your information.
  • We never sell or share your data for marketing.
  • We follow GDPR-aware handling and honour data-subject requests.

3. Access controls

Access to client accounts and data is restricted to authorised team members on a need-to-know basis, with activity logged. Our internal CRM enforces role-based permissions so each team member sees only what their role requires.

4. Confidentiality

We treat your business information, target lists, and messaging as confidential and use them only to deliver your campaign.

5. Incident response

If a security issue affecting your data occurs, we will investigate promptly and notify you where appropriate. Report any concern to vibhanshu@techinrent.com.
